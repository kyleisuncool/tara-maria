# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

People seeking healing services: curious first-time visitors exploring what Tara-Maria offers, and returning clients looking to book or stay connected. They arrive in a reflective or searching state — not transactional. They want to feel seen before they decide to engage.

## Product Purpose

A marketing and presence site for Tara-Maria, a healer. It exists to communicate who she is, what she offers, and invite connection. Starting as a single-page site. Future features include booking, an upcoming events calendar, and a newsletter signup. Some of these may be powered by third-party API integrations.

## Brand Personality

Calm, grounded, earthy, welcoming, inviting, and whimsical. A visitor should leave feeling like they've encountered something genuine and quietly alive — not performed or commercial. Whimsy is a texture, not a costume.

## Anti-references

None locked in yet. Likely to emerge as design evolves. Avoid:
- Generic spa clichés (stock candle photos, cursive-on-beige, teal accents)
- Loud new-age aesthetics (purple gradients, heavy crystal imagery)
- Trendy wellness-influencer pastels

## Design Principles

1. **Presence over polish.** The site should feel inhabited and real, not like a template. Personality comes through in specificity, not ornamentation.
2. **Let stillness lead.** Negative space and unhurried rhythm carry the healing quality. Don't fill; breathe.
3. **Warmth in the details.** Whimsy lives in small, surprising touches, not in loud decoration.
4. **Clarity as care.** Information about services, events, and how to book should be effortless to find. Making things easy is an act of hospitality.
5. **Earn the scroll.** Each section of the page should justify its existence. Nothing decorative that doesn't also serve.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA as a baseline. Ensure sufficient contrast for calm, low-saturation palettes. Support reduced motion preferences.
